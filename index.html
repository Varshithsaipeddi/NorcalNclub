01 / ABOUT

A Crew, Not a Club.

We're drivers first. Built around the cars Hyundai N gave us — and the canyons, coast, and tracks that make Northern California one of the best places on Earth to drive them.

From Bay Area cars-and-coffee to Sierra runs, Laguna Seca lapping days, and dyno nights — NorCal N Club is where bone-stock dailies park next to fully built track weapons, and everyone walks away with new friends, better lines, and fresh photos.

  • ○ Monthly meets across the Bay, Sacramento, and beyond
  • ○ Group canyon drives — Skyline, Hwy 9, Hwy 1, Mines Road
  • ○ HPDE / track day caravans — Laguna, Sonoma, Thunderhill
  • ○ Tech sessions, alignment days, group buys
